The Problem

Distance creates a
"Trust Vacuum."

For decades, NRIs have entrusted their most valuable Indian assets to third parties — with no visibility, no accountability, and no recourse when things go wrong.

01 🏚️

Encroachment Fear

Vacant plots are prime targets. A squatter can erect a temporary shed in 48 hours — but a traditional verification report takes 30 days.

"Is someone squatting on my vacant plot right now?"
02 ⚖️

Legal Rot

Missed tax payments, expired documents, and unchecked legal challenges silently erode your ownership — until it's too late to fight back.

"Is my title deed still valid? Did I miss a tax payment?"
03 🔒

Liquidity Trap

Even when NRIs want to sell, buyers 8,000 miles away don't trust unverified documents. Assets that should be liquid are frozen in doubt.

"I can't sell because buyers don't trust my documents from abroad."
